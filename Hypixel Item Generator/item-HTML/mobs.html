Scatha

The Scatha is a type of Mob that can be found in the Crystal Hollows while mining

 

LOCATION

The [Lv10] Scatha can be found in the Crystal Hollows while mining. SkyBlock sprite entities worm.png Worms have a 1/600 chance to spawn when breaking Minecraft items Hard Stone in a tunnel, and a 20% chance of turning into a [Lv10] Scatha. When the player spawns a Scatha, a countdown of 30 seconds starts until the player can spawn another Scatha.

Upon the [Lv10] Scatha spawning, a squelching sound plays, accompanied by a message in chat: "You hear the sound of something approaching..."

 

BEHAVIOUR

The [Lv10] Scatha will slither away from the player upon spawning in. Each hit from the player removes 1 segment and 1 SkyBlock icons health.pngHealth from the [Lv10] Scatha, and the 10th hit destroys the head, killing it.
